## Overview

The Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate provided a practical introduction to the core concepts of cybersecurity through hands-on learning and guided labs. The program covered foundational security principles, networking, Linux, SQL, Python, security operations, incident response, and risk management while emphasizing real-world security workflows.

Completing this program helped reinforce my decision to pursue a career in cybersecurity and gave me a strong foundation before earning CompTIA Security+.
