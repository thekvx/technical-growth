## Overview

CompTIA Security+ is an entry-level cybersecurity certification that validates foundational knowledge across core security concepts, networking, identity management, risk management, cryptography, and incident response.

Preparing for this certification strengthened my understanding of the principles that support modern cybersecurity and provided a solid foundation for continued learning in both Information Technology and Cybersecurity.
