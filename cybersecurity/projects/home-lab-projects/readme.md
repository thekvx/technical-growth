# 🖥️ Home Lab Projects

Home labs provide a safe environment to explore new technologies, reinforce technical concepts, and gain practical experience through experimentation. They allow me to build, configure, troubleshoot, and document systems while developing the hands-on skills used throughout Information Technology and Cybersecurity.

This section serves as a collection of the home lab environments I build throughout my learning journey. Each project documents the goals, technologies used, configuration process, challenges encountered, and lessons learned while demonstrating the practical application of real-world concepts.

# 💡 Project Philosophy

Home labs provide the freedom to experiment, make mistakes, and learn without affecting production systems. They allow me to explore technologies at my own pace while reinforcing the concepts I'm studying through hands-on experience. Rather than simply following tutorials, I focus on understanding how systems work together, documenting the process, and applying those lessons to future projects.

This section isn't intended to showcase completed environments alone. Instead, it documents the practical experience I'm building as I continue working toward a career in cybersecurity.
