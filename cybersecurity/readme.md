# 🛡️ Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the field I'm working toward professionally. Building upon a foundation in Information Technology, my focus is on understanding how organizations protect systems, detect threats, respond to incidents, and continuously improve their security posture.

This section documents the cybersecurity concepts I've studied, the practical skills I'm developing, and the hands-on experience I'm gaining through certification studies, labs, and continued practice. Everything included here reflects my personal learning journey as I continue growing toward a career in cybersecurity.

---

# 📚 Areas of Study

### 🛡️ [Security Operations](sercurity-operations/readme.md)

Learning the day-to-day responsibilities of defending systems, monitoring security events, documenting findings, and supporting security operations within an organization.

### 🎯 [Threat Detection](threat-detection/readme.md)

Building practical experience with the security tools used to analyze systems, investigate events, monitor activity, and strengthen defensive capabilities.

### 📂 Projects

A collection of cybersecurity projects, labs, and practical exercises that demonstrate the concepts I've studied and the skills I've developed through hands-on practice.

# 💡 Learning Philosophy

Cybersecurity isn't just about stopping attacks. It's about understanding how technology, people, and processes work together to reduce risk and respond effectively when incidents occur. As I continue learning, I focus on building practical knowledge through hands-on labs, technical documentation, and continual study.

This section isn't intended to be a complete cybersecurity reference. Instead, it documents the knowledge I've personally built as I continue developing the skills needed for a career in cybersecurity.
