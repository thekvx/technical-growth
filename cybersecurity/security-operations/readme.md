# 🛡️ Security Operations

Security Operations is the area of cybersecurity that interests me most and the direction I'm intentionally working toward. It focuses on monitoring systems, identifying suspicious activity, investigating security events, and helping organizations protect their people, systems, and data.

This section documents the security operations concepts I've studied, the practical skills I'm developing, and the hands-on experience I'm gaining through certification studies, labs, and continued practice. Everything included here reflects my personal learning journey as I continue building the knowledge needed for a career in cybersecurity.

---

# 📚 Areas of Study

### 👀 [Security Monitoring](security-monitoring/readme.md)

Learning how organizations monitor systems, review alerts, and identify activity that may require investigation.

---

### 🚨 Alert Triage

Building an understanding of how security alerts are prioritized, investigated, documented, and escalated when appropriate.

---

### 📄 Investigation & Documentation

Developing structured approaches to documenting findings, communicating observations, and maintaining clear records throughout the investigation process.

---

### 🛡️ Defensive Security

Learning how layered security controls, security best practices, and defensive strategies work together to reduce organizational risk.

---

### 📈 Continuous Improvement

Building the habit of reviewing investigations, identifying lessons learned, and continuously improving both technical knowledge and defensive practices.

---

# 💡 Learning Philosophy

One of the reasons I'm drawn to Security Operations is that it combines technical knowledge with investigation and problem solving. I enjoy understanding how systems behave, recognizing when something doesn't look right, and following the evidence to better understand what happened.

This section isn't intended to be a guide to Security Operations. Instead, it documents the knowledge I'm personally building as I continue working toward a career in cybersecurity.
