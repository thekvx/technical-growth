# 🔐 Identity Security

Identity Security focuses on protecting user identities, managing access to organizational resources, and ensuring that the right people have the appropriate level of access at the right time. As organizations continue adopting cloud technologies, identity has become one of the most important aspects of modern cybersecurity.

This section documents the identity security concepts I've studied, the practical skills I'm developing, and the hands-on experience I'm gaining through certification studies, labs, and continued practice. Everything included here reflects my personal learning journey as I continue building the knowledge needed for a career in cybersecurity.

---

Continuing to strengthen my understanding of identity security by studying current best practices, evolving technologies, and the role identity plays in reducing organizational risk.

# 💡 Learning Philosophy

Identity has become one of the most important security boundaries in modern organizations. Rather than viewing identity as simply user accounts and passwords, I focus on understanding how authentication, authorization, and access management work together to help protect systems, users, and data.

This section isn't intended to be a complete reference for Identity Security. Instead, it documents the knowledge I'm personally building as I continue working toward a career in cybersecurity.
